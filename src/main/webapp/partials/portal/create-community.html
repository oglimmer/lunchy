First Office properties
This is the name of your first office, usually this contains the city name, if you don't know what to put here use something like HQ.
To get lat/lng for your first office go to google-maps and find your office location. Then get the coordinates from the address bar, it looks like https://www.google.de/maps/@50.1083762,8.6870005,15z - this would mean: lat:50.1083762, lng:8.6870005 and zoomfactor: 15
Zoom factor for google maps. A 13 is always a good start.
